What kind of work do you take on? +

Custom platforms and systems — research and operations tools, internal software, multi-tenant SaaS, and AI-assisted workflows — for organizations that need software they can rely on long term. I take on the architecture and the build, not isolated tickets.

Do you handle the whole system, or just part of it? +

The whole system — architecture, full-stack development, AI integration, and the infrastructure it runs on. You work with one engineer who owns the outcome end to end.

What if you're unavailable mid-project? +

Continuity is built into how I work: clean documentation, version-controlled code, and reproducible, deployable infrastructure. The systems I build aren't locked in my head — they can be picked up, handed off, or maintained by another engineer without starting over.

What does a project cost? +

Every project is scoped individually based on requirements, complexity, and timeline. After an initial discussion, I'll provide a clear proposal with scope, deliverables, and pricing.

How do you handle AI features responsibly? +

I build AI on controlled-response architectures: it answers from approved information and hands off to a human when something falls outside its scope, rather than guessing. AI should reduce risk, not add it.
